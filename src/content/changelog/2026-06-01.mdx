We've made two upgrades to the Unkey dashboard: a reorganized navigation, and a clearer name for your APIs.

## A new navigation

We've reorganized the sidebar so everything in your workspace is in one place. Your projects, keyspaces, ratelimits, logs, and settings are all a click away.

## APIs are now Keyspaces (APIs)

We've renamed APIs to Keyspaces (APIs). A keyspace is the container your keys live in, and the new name says exactly that.

- You'll find your APIs under **Keyspaces (APIs)** in the sidebar.

- Your API ID is unchanged, so your integrations keep working. You can continue to use the same `api_…` value as before, now shown next to the KeySpace ID on each keyspace's settings page.

Read more in the [Keyspaces documentation](https://www.unkey.com/docs/platform/apis/overview).
